How to play: Players take turns. On your turn a disc appears at the outer edge of your quadrant — use the slider below the board to position it along your line, then drag anywhere on the board in the direction you want to shoot (further drag = more power) and release to flick. Land in the centre hole for 20, or score by ring (15 / 10 / 5). Discs knocked into the ditch are out. If your opponent has a disc on the board you must hit one (directly or off your own discs) or your shot is removed; on an open board your shot must reach the centre 20 zone. Highest total after all discs are played wins.
